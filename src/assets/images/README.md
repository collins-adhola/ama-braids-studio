# Images

Add your photos here and update the corresponding components:

- **hero.jpeg** — main hero image (portrait or landscape, client with braids, high quality)
- **portrait.jpeg** — your professional portrait photo for the About section
- **gallery-1.jpeg** through **gallery-6.jpeg** — client work photos for the Instagram/Gallery section

Once images are added, replace placeholder divs in components:

```html
<!-- HeroComponent: replace placeholder div with -->
<img src="assets/images/hero.jpeg" alt="AMA Hair Studio — Premium Braiding" />

<!-- AboutComponent: replace placeholder div with -->
<img src="assets/images/portrait.jpeg" alt="AMA — Your Hair Artist" />

<!-- InstagramFeedComponent: replace placeholder tiles with -->
<img src="assets/images/gallery-1.jpeg" alt="Knotless Braids" />
```

Recommended image specs:
- hero.jpeg: 1200×900px minimum
- portrait.jpeg: 800×1000px minimum, portrait orientation
- gallery-*.jpeg: 600×600px square crops work best
